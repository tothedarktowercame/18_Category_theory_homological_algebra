LÃ©o Hubert extends the classical Dold--Kan correspondence from simplicial abelian groups to abelian presheaves on test categories $A$ satisfying a compatibility between homology and weak equivalences. Under this hypothesis the homotopy category of abelian presheaves on $A$ matches the non-negative derived category of abelian groups, via a Quillen equivalence built from a new model structure. Joyal's $\Theta$ satisfies the compatibility, delivering a conceptual bridge between $\Theta$-spaces and chain complexes.

\subsection*{Synopsis}
Brown, Golasi≈Ñski, Porter, and Tonks generalize equivariant homotopy theory of crossed complexes from discrete to topological groups. Using homotopy coherence and Eilenberg-Zilber theory, they classify equivariant maps and describe weak equivariant homotopy types of mapping spaces.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Extends equivariant crossed complex theory to topological groups.
  \item Utilizes homotopy coherence via Eilenberg-Zilber results.
  \item Provides classification of equivariant maps and mapping space homotopy types.
\end{enumerate}

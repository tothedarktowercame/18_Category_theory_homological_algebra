\subsection*{Synopsis}
Alves Neto, Batista, and MÃ©ndez analyse partial representations of a finite group $G$ as a multifusion category. They classify simples, describe tensor products, connect to global representations of subgroups ("Christmas Tree" theorem), and prove an embedding result for partial representations of subgroups into those of $G$ when $G$ is abelian ("Matryoshka" theorem).
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Shows the category of partial representations is multifusion and classifies simple objects.
  \item Describes tensor products via subgroup representations (Christmas Tree theorem).
  \item Proves embedding results for abelian groups (Matryoshka theorem).
\end{enumerate}
